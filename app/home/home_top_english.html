Census Date for Semester 2 is just around the corner.

Published on July 27, 2016.

The Census date for Semester 2 is 31/8/2016.  This date is the deadline to take key actions, such as drop a class or cancel enrolment without financial penalty.  

Here are some things to consider before the Census Date:

How are you going with your studies?

Have you been able to dedicate enough time to each unit?

Will you be able to continue dedicating enough time to each unit?

If you have any question regarding the Census Date please get in contact with us: help@bsharps.edu.au

Mid Year Applications Now Open.

Published on June 3, 2016.

Why wait until 2017 to start studying?  Apply now!

Have a look at undergraduate and postgraduate degrees that commence mid-year, there are over 220 programs to choose from.  Just remember that not all courses offer mid-year enrolment.

The deadline for mid-year enrolment is July 15, 2016, but applications will continue to be accepted for programs that still have places available.
